FAQS

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to help users understand the risks associated with cardiovascular disease.

How do we achieve this purpose?

This is achieved by through a discussion forum as well as BMI calculator.

What are the benefits if I sign up as a user of this website?

Signing up as a user allows you to track changes in your BMI calculations to see progress on your health

What is a BMI calculator and how is it calculated?

BMI calculators determine the body mass index of a person based on their height and weight. The formula used for BMI is: mass/height*2 for metric systems & mass/height*2 x 703
