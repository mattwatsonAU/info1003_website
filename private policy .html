Privacy Policy



This Privacy Policy applies to all pages on the healthy.me site. If you have any general queries about this site, please contact: healthy.me

Users of this site are authorized to expect that any information gained as a result of using it, will be handled according to the New South Wales Privacy and Personal Information Protection Act 1988.


What information do we collect from people who visit our website?

When you enter pages on the healthyme site, information about the following is automatically taken:

  • the IP address
  • the date and time of access
  • the pages accessed
  • the type of browser used
  • the address of your server


When registering for our website, you are asked to enter your:


  • Name
  • Email address
  • Age
  • Weight
  • Goal weight


When do we collect information?

We collect information from you access the website, register, use the BMI calculator and respond to poll or surveys.


How do we use your information?

We use it to personalise users experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content in which you are most interested. The collected information during each site visit assists us in making the website more user friendly. The information is strictly confidential and will not be disclosed without consent.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This dose not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you.non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.


Will this policy be changed over time?

Yes there maybe changes over time due to the constant modification of privacy regulations for online services. Any changes in policy will be posted on the home page straight away and members will be notified via email by 5 working days.


If you have any general privacy enquiry please contact: privacy@healthy.me
